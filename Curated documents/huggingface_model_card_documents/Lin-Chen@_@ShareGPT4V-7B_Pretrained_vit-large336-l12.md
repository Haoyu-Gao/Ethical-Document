## Model details

**Model type:**
This is the vision tower of ShareGPT4V-7B fine-tuned with our [ShareGPT4V dataset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/Lin-Chen/ShareGPT4V).

**Model date:**
This vision tower was trained in Nov 2023.
